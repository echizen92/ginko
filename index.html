Ordinals and Inscription

Ordinals and inscriptions are mechanisms by which satoshis, units of bitcoin, are identified, tracked, and inscribed with information on the Bitcoin blockchain. The process of inscription inscribes unique information associated with a satoshi that differentiates it from other satoshis. After inscription, a satoshi could then be considered non-fungible with other satoshis.
